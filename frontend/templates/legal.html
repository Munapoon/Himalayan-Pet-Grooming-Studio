Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Your privacy is important to us. It is Himalayan Pet Studio's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website.

Data Collection

We only ask for personal information (such as name, email, and phone number) when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent.

Data Protection

We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service. What data we store, we’ll protect within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft.

Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Service Refunds

If you are not satisfied with our grooming service, please let us know before leaving the studio. We will do our best to correct it immediately. Once the pet has left the premises, refunds are generally not provided.

Product Refunds

Products purchased through our shop can be returned within 7 days of purchase if they are unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt. Perishable pet food and treats are not returnable.

Booking Rules

Last Updated: April 2026

Cancellation Policy

Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the appointment may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Late Arrivals

If you are more than 15 minutes late for your appointment, we may need to reschedule or shorten the grooming session to avoid delaying other appointments. Fees may still apply.

Pet Vaccinations

For the safety of all pets at our studio, we require all grooming clients to be up-to-date on their core vaccinations (Rabies, DHLPP). Proof of vaccination may be requested.
