About Our Studio

Himalayan Pet Grooming Studio

Premium grooming and specialized care for your beloved pets in the heart of the Himalayas. We treat every animal with love and expertise.

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Pokhara, Nepal, Himalayan Pet Grooming Studio began with a simple mission: to provide world-class grooming services for pets in the Himalayan region.

What started as a small grooming salon has grown into a comprehensive pet care destination, serving hundreds of happy pets and their families. Our team of certified groomers combines traditional care with modern techniques to ensure your pet looks and feels their best.

We believe that every pet deserves professional care in a comfortable, stress-free environment. That's why we've designed our studio to be a welcoming space where pets can relax and enjoy their grooming experience.
